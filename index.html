Why Veridoc

Renewals shouldn't live in your head.

A license, a DEA registration every three years, board recertification, CME deadlines — often across more than one state. Miss one and you can't prescribe, can't practice, and your next credentialing packet stalls.

The usual way

  • A spreadsheet you update once and forget
  • Calendar reminders set a year out, then snoozed
  • The renewal notice buried in your inbox
  • Scrambling for documents when a new employer credentials you

With Veridoc

  • Every credential in one place, with its real expiration date
  • Reminders at 60, 30, and 14 days — you can't miss them
  • A clear status for each one: current, expiring, or expired
  • An audit-ready summary to send in one click
